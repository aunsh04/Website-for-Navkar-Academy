About Navkar Academy:
Navkar Academy Private Limited was set up in 2015 to provide coaching class services for various commerce fields including Professional Courses like CA – CS and Undergraduate courses. Navkar Academy has taken franchise from the Navkar Institute located at Ahmedabad and opened various coaching centres in Mumbai and other locations for professional courses like Chartered Accountancy , Company Secretary. Navkar Institute has provided a full fledged support such as necessary guidance, training, academic assistance, study material, necessary assistance for conducting tests, etc. to Navkar Academy. We are currently situated at three regions in Mumbai namely - Mulund, Borivali and Santacruz and are planning to start new coaching centres at other locations as well such as Navi Mumbai, Andheri, Ghatkopar, Vashi, Kalyan, Dombivali, Bhayander, Vasai etc. Navkar Academy is also planning to associate with various colleges & classes. Presently Navkar Academy will start at Thakur, Kandivali.
